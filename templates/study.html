Answer 6 questions (name, country, school, channel) and Study is live. Then just type: "Hey Study, I need to prepare for my exam. I have 30 days."

❓ FAQ

Do I need to know how to code?

No. The setup script does everything. If you can use a terminal, you can install Study.

Can I use it for any exam?

Yes. Study adapts to your school system. If yours is missing, you can add it — it's open source.

Does it work on Windows?

Yes. Use python setup.py. Works on Windows, Mac, and Linux.

Is it really free? What's the catch?

No catch. Study is MIT licensed. You only pay for DeepSeek API tokens (~$3-5/month). Study runs locally on your machine — no HermesBro servers involved.

Why is it free when other HermesBro products are paid?

Education is a right. Tommy built Study while preparing for his own exams and decided to give it to the world. Other HermesBro products (AI accounting, trading bots) are professional tools for businesses.
